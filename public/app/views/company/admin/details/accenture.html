About Accenture:

  • Accenture is a global professional services company providing management consulting, technology services, and outsourcing.
  • Founded in 1989, Accenture has grown to become one of the largest consulting firms in the world.
  • Accenture operates in over 51 countries and has more than 738,000 employees.
  • Headquartered in Dublin, Ireland, Accenture offers services in various domains, including management consulting, technology services, digital services, outsourcing, industry-specific solutions, and interactive.

Recruitment Process:

  • Accenture conducts 4-5 rounds to select freshers as software developers/engineers in their organization.
  • Aptitude Test
  • Technical Interview
  • HR Interview

Academic and Graduation Criteria:

  • Minimum 60% or above in B.Tech, Class X, and XII.
  • No active/current backlogs at the time of the interview.

Round 1: Cognitive Test

  • The cognitive test is conducted on the SHL Platform.
  • The online test consists of quantitative aptitude, logical reasoning, and CS fundamentals questions.
  • The test has a total of 90 questions to be attempted within 90 minutes.
  • The sectional and sub-sectional cut-off is set for this test.
  • Those who clear this test become eligible for the second test.

Round 2: Coding Assessment

  • The coding assessment is scheduled on the same day as the cognitive test.
  • The scores obtained in this test are considered for the final selection decisions at the zonal level.
  • Candidates are required to solve two coding questions within 45 minutes.
  • Those who clear this round proceed to the technical interview round.

Round 3: Communication Test

  • In this round, candidates are asked to read the given statements.
  • Those who clear this round proceed to the technical interview round.

Round 4: Technical Interview Round

  • Duration: 15-25 minutes
  • Questions/topics covered:
    • Tell me about yourself (In the introduction, give a brief about your project)
    • OOPs (Polymorphism, Access Specifier, How can we achieve access specifier in C++, Constructors and their types, destructors)
    • Tell me about software testing
    • Students will be expected to write codes in the interview.

Round 5: HR Round

  • Questions asked in the HR round:
    • Introduce yourself
    • What are your strengths?
    • Why did you choose Engineering?
    • Why do you want to join Accenture?
    • Location Preference
