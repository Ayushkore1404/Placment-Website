About KPMG:

KPMG's written test consists of two sections: Numerical Ability and Verbal Ability. Here are the topics covered in each section:

Numerical Ability:

  • Data Interpretation
  • Algebra Graphs
  • Geometry Probability
  • Time and Work Time, Speed Distance
  • Percentages
  • Profit and Loss

Verbal Ability:

  • Spotting Errors
  • Reading Comprehension
  • Passage Idioms
  • Synonyms and Antonyms
  • Fill in the blanks
  • Paragraph completion
  • Sentence Completion
  • Vocabulary
