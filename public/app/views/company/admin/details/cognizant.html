About Cognizant:

  • Cognizant is an American multinational corporation founded in 1994 by Kumar Mahadeva. It has over 355,000 employees in 40+ countries. The company is headquartered in New Jersey, USA.
  • Cognizant provides intelligent, digital, and tactical solutions that transform the way businesses operate without interrupting business as usual. Their services include information technology, information security, consulting, ITO (Information Technology Outsourcing), and BPO (Business Process Outsourcing) services.

Domain of Work:

  • Consulting Services
  • Technology Services
  • Digital Services
  • Outsourcing
  • Industry-Specific Solutions
  • Cloud Solutions

Recruitment Process:

  • Cognizant conducts 3-4 rounds to select freshers as SDE (Software Development Engineer) in their organization.
  • Aptitude Test
  • Programming
  • HR Interview

Academic and Graduation Criteria:

  • Minimum 60% or above in B.Tech, Class X, and XII.
  • No active/current backlogs at the time of the interview.

Round 1: Cognitive Test

  • The cognitive test is conducted on the Superset Platform. It is an online test that consists of sections such as Quantitative Aptitude, Logical Reasoning, Verbal Ability, and Automata Fix.
  • The sectional and sub-sectional cutoffs for this test are not disclosed.
  • Those who clear this test become eligible for the programming test.

Round 2: Coding Assessment

  • In this round, candidates are required to solve coding questions within the given time.
  • Those who clear this round proceed to the technical interview round.

Round 3: Technical Interview Round

  • Questions/topics covered:
    • Questions on OOPs concepts
    • Programming questions
    • Questions on Data Structures
    • Questions from the resume
    • Puzzles

Round 4: HR Round

  • Questions asked in the HR round:
    • Introduce yourself
    • What are your strengths?
    • Why Cognizant?
    • Why do you want to join Cognizant?
    • Why should we hire you?
    • What are your achievements in life?
