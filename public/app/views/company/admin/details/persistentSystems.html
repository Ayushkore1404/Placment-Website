About Persistent Systems:

Persistent Systems Limited is a global company specializing in software products, services, and technology innovation. They offer complete product life cycle services and deliver services across all stages of the product life cycle. Persistent Systems operates in various domains of work, including Digital Strategy & Design, Software Product Engineering, CX Transformation, Cloud & Infrastructure, Intelligent Automation, Enterprise IT Security, Data & Analytics, Application Development and Management, and Enterprise Integration.

Recruitment Process:

  • Persistent Systems offers pre-placement offers through their Martian program.
  • The Martian Program is not limited to colleges and is conducted in the whole Nagpur region.
  • Persistent Systems offers three types of packages: 5LPA, 7.8LPA, and 9LPA.
  • Academic and Graduation Criteria: 60% or 6.0 CGPA throughout 10th, 12th, and UG/PG.
  • No active/current Backlogs.
  • Round 1: Online Test on SHL Platform (Quantitative aptitude, Logical Reasoning, CS Fundamentals, and 2 coding questions).
  • Round 2: Technical Interview Round 1.
  • Round 3: Technical Interview Round 2.
  • Round 4: In-office face-to-face interview (HR questions).
