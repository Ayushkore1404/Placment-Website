About Capgemini:

Capgemini is a global company offering consulting services, technology services, digital transformation, outsourcing and managed services, industry-specific solutions, software engineering, and data analytics. With headquarters in Paris, France, Capgemini has a vast workforce of over 738,000 employees across 50+ countries.

Recruitment Process:

  • Capgemini conducts 3-4 rounds to select freshers as Software Engineers in their organization.
  • Academic and Graduation Criteria: 60% or above in B.Tech, Class X, and XII. No active/current backlogs at the time of the interview.
  • Round 1: Technical or Skills Assessment
  • Round 2: Coding Assessment
  • Round 3: Communication Test
  • Round 4: Technical Interview Round
  • Round 5: HR Round
