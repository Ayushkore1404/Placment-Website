About Cybage Software:

Cybage Software conducts a written test and a technical test as part of its recruitment process. The written test includes questions from mathematics (quant) and English. The mathematics questions are moderate, covering topics such as LCM and HCF, divisibility, number fraction, algebra, ratio and proportion, profit and loss, time, speed, and distance, and more. The English questions are a bit difficult, covering topics like reading comprehension, grammar, para jumbles, antonyms, synonyms, idioms and phrases, error identification, sentence correction, tenses, and articles.

Written Test:

  • Total of 30 questions
  • Topics for Mathematics:
    • LCM and HCF
    • Divisibility
    • Number Fraction
    • Averages
    • Algebra
    • Ratio and Proportion
    • Profit and loss
    • Time, speed, and distance
    • Inverse
    • Simple and Compound Interest
  • Topics for English:
    • Reading Comprehension
    • Grammar
    • Para Jumbles
    • Antonyms
    • Synonyms
    • Idioms and Phrases
    • Error Identification
    • Sentence correction
    • Tenses & Articles

Technical Test:

The technical interview at Cybage Software is a bit tough. It includes questions related to Java, JSP, and development.
