About PwC:

PwC's exam consists of three sections: Numerical Test, Verbal Ability, and Logical Reasoning. Here are the topics covered in each section:

Numerical Test:

  • Algebra
  • Time & Work
  • Time, Speed & Distance
  • Arithmetic
  • Percentages
  • Profit & Loss
  • Geometry

Verbal Ability:

  • Reading Comprehension
  • Fill-in-the-blanks
  • Synonyms
  • Antonyms
  • Paragraph Completion
  • Sentence Completion
  • Vocabulary

Logical Reasoning:

  • Data Interpretation
  • Logical Reasoning
  • Puzzles
  • Graphs, Charts, and Tables
