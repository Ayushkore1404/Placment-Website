About Siemens:

The written test round in Siemens varies in time duration for each section. The difficulty level of the exam is moderate to difficult, and there is no negative marking. The syllabus for the Siemens online test includes the following sections:

Quantitative Aptitude:

This section consists of questions from topics such as time and work, speed and distance, algebra, arithmetic, profit and loss, percentages, geometry, etc. The questions are of moderate level.

Analytical Reasoning:

This section consists of logical reasoning and data interpretation questions. It includes topics like puzzles, blood relations, directions, coding & decoding, number series, and data sufficiency.

Verbal Ability:

This section includes short reading comprehension passages and questions about basic grammar, such as fill-in-the-blanks, paragraph completion, synonyms, antonyms, vocabulary, sentence correction, etc.

Technical:

This section involves questions from core subjects of Computer Science and Electronics, including topics like Operating Systems, C & C++ programming, and Computer Networks. This section is often tough and requires good preparation. Candidates with strong fundamentals can excel in this section easily.
