About P360:

  • P360 is an advanced pharma software company specializing in business technology development and implementation for pharmaceutical and life sciences companies worldwide. Founded in 2007 by Anupam Nandwana and Edward Vaz, P360 has more than 200 employees in 3+ countries. The company is headquartered in Piscataway, New Jersey, United States.

Domain of Work:

  • Business Technology Development
  • Technology Services

Recruitment Process:

  • P360 conducts 3-4 rounds to select freshers as SDE (Software Development Engineer) in their organization.
  • Aptitude Test
  • Technical Interview
  • HR Interview

Academic and Graduation Criteria:

  • Minimum 60% or above in B.Tech, Class X, and XII.
  • No active/current backlogs at the time of the interview.

Round 1: Aptitude Test

  • The aptitude test assesses the candidate's mathematical and logical reasoning skills. It is conducted to evaluate their problem-solving abilities.

Round 2: Coding Assessment

  • The coding assessment is scheduled on the same day as the aptitude test. It consists of two coding questions that need to be solved within the given time. Clearing this round qualifies the candidate for the technical interview round.

Round 3: Technical Interview Round

  • Time: 25-30 minutes
  • Questions/topics covered:
    • Tell me about yourself.
    • Tell me about software testing.
    • Students will be expected to write code during the interview.
    • Questions on Data Structures and Algorithms, DBMS, Operating Systems, Networking, OOPs concepts.
    • Questions on personal projects.
    • Questions on the resume.

Round 4: HR Round

  • Questions asked in the HR round:
    • Introduce yourself.
    • What are your strengths and weaknesses?
    • Why did you choose Engineering?
    • How do you see yourself in five years from now?
    • What are your short-term and long-term goals?
