Founded in 2000

About GlobalLogic:

  • GlobalLogic is a leader in digital product engineering.
  • The company helps clients design and build innovative products, platforms, and digital experiences.
  • They operate in various industries, including automotive, communications, financial services, healthcare, media and entertainment, manufacturing, semiconductor, and technology.
  • GlobalLogic is headquartered in Silicon Valley and operates design studios and engineering centers globally.
  • They focus on integrating strategic design, complex engineering, and vertical industry expertise to accelerate the transition of clients into digital businesses.

Recruitment Process:

  • Students from Computer Technology, ETC, Electronics, and Information Technology are allowed to apply for the drive.
  • Round 1: Aptitude Test and Programming Questions
  • Round 2: First Technical Interview
  • Round 3: Second Technical Interview
  • Round 4: Group Discussion
  • Round 5: Managerial and HR Round (also an elimination round)
