About TCS:

TCS Placement Papers are divided into two rounds: Round 1 and Round 2. Here are the sections included in each round:

Round 1:

  • Traits:
    • Total Questions: 1 (image selection question)
    • Time Allotted: 75 mins (shared)
  • Numerical Ability:
    • Total Questions: 20
    • Time Allotted: 75 mins (shared)
    • Topics: LCM and HCF, Divisibility, Number Fraction, Averages
  • Verbal Ability:
    • Total Questions: 25
    • Time Allotted: 75 mins (shared)
    • Topics: Reading Comprehension, Prepositions, Para Jumbles, Fill in the Blanks, Antonyms, Synonyms, Idioms and Phrases, Error Identification, Sentence Correction, Tenses & Articles
  • Reasoning Ability:
    • Total Questions: 20
    • Time Allotted: 75 mins (shared)
    • Topics: Data Sufficiency, Analogy, Syllogism, Odd Man Out, Seating Arrangement

Round 2:

  • Advanced Quantitative Ability:
    • Total Questions: 10
    • Time Allotted: 35 mins (shared)
    • Topics: LCM and HCF, Divisibility, Number Fraction, Averages, Algebra, Ratio and Proportion, Profit and loss, Time, speed, and distance, Inverse, Simple and Compound Interest
  • Advanced Reasoning Ability:
    • Total Questions: 10
    • Time Allotted: 35 mins (shared)
    • Topics: Blood Relation, Statements & Conclusion, Data Sufficiency, Analogy, Logical Sequence, Syllogism, Odd Man Out, Seating Arrangement, Coding Patterns
  • Advanced Coding:
    • Total Questions: 2
    • Time Allotted: 55 mins
    • The number of questions is 1 in 30 mins, which is simple and has to be coded in the in-built online compiler in TCS Online Test Papers.
