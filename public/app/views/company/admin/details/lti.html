About LTI:

  • LTI (Larsen & Toubro Infotech) is a global IT services and solutions company headquartered in Mumbai, India.
  • LTI is a subsidiary of Larsen & Toubro Limited (L&T), a leading engineering and construction conglomerate in India.
  • Founded in 1997, LTI has grown to become one of the fastest-growing IT services companies in the world.
  • LTI offers a wide range of services and solutions to clients across various industries, including banking and financial services, insurance, manufacturing, retail, healthcare, and more.

Domain of Work:

  • Application Development and Maintenance
  • Digital Transformation
  • Infrastructure Management Services
  • Consulting and Advisory Services
  • Data Analytics

Recruitment Process:

  • LTI conducts 3-4 rounds to select freshers as software engineers in their organization.
  • Aptitude Test
  • Technical Interview
  • HR Interview

Academic and Graduation Criteria:

  • Minimum 60% or above in B.Tech, Class X, and XII.
  • No active/current backlogs at the time of the interview.

Round 1: Technical or Skills Assessment

  • Depending on the position the candidate is applying for, they may be required to complete a technical or skills assessment.
  • The assessment may involve tests, coding exercises, case studies, or other assessments designed to evaluate knowledge and capabilities in specific areas.
  • The assessment includes:
    • Quantitative Aptitude MCQ
    • Logical Reasoning MCQ
    • Verbal Reasoning MCQ
    • Technical MCQ
    • Paragraph Writing
    • Psychometric Questions

Round 2: Coding Assessment

  • In this round, candidates are required to solve two coding questions within 45 minutes.

Round 3: Technical Interview Round

  • Duration: 15-25 minutes
  • Questions/topics covered:
    • Tell me about yourself (In the introduction, give a brief about your project)
    • Candidates will be expected to write codes in the interview.
    • Questions about previous projects
    • Questions from the resume
    • Discussion on the latest technologies
    • What is the difference between function overloading and function overriding in C++?
    • What is a virtual function in C++?

Round 4: HR Round

  • Questions asked in the HR round:
    • Introduce yourself
    • What are your strengths?
    • How do you see yourself after five years from now?
    • Are you willing to relocate?
    • What are your hobbies?
