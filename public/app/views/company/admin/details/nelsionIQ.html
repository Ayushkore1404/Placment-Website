About NielsenIQ:

  • NielsenIQ, formerly known as Nielsen Holdings, is a global measurement and data analytics company. It was founded in 1923 by Arthur C. Nielsen Sr. and has since become a leading provider of market research, consumer insights, and media measurement services. NielsenIQ operates in over 100 countries and has more than 30,000 employees worldwide. The company is headquartered in Chicago, USA.

Domain of Work:

  • Retail Measurement
  • Consumer Insights
  • Media Measurement
  • E-commerce Measurement
  • Marketing Effectiveness

Recruitment Process:

  • NielsenIQ conducts 2-3 rounds to select freshers as Software Engineers in their organization.
  • Aptitude Test
  • Technical Interview

Academic and Graduation Criteria:

  • Minimum 60% or above in B.Tech, Class X, and XII.
  • No active/current backlogs at the time of the interview.

Round 1: Online Coding Test

  • The online test is conducted on the Codility platform and consists of 3 coding questions (2 easy and 1 medium level). It is based on Dynamic Programming. Practice standard questions from GeeksforGeeks related to Data Structures and Algorithms to solve these questions easily.

Round 2: Technical Interview Round

  • The interview is divided into 4 sections:
  • Section 1: Introduction and general questions from resumes and projects.
  • Section 2: Coding round with questions related to arrays and strings:
    • Given two strings, check if the first string is the reverse of the other.
    • Given a string, find all the repeating characters in the string.
    • Given a number N and an array of 1 to N-1 elements, find the missing number.
  • Section 3: DBMS concepts and questions related to normalization.
  • Section 4: Standard puzzles.

Round 3: HR Round

  • Introduction
  • Strengths
  • Why did you choose Engineering?
  • Location Preference
